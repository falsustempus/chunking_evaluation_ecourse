課程目標   Learning Objectives
Molecular Genetics is the study of genes, as well as to manipulate their function in order to explore important biological questions.
From introductory to advanced concepts, this course will teach students how genes are expressed to control biological functions from different aspects of cellular activities to growth development, as well as how gene deregulation contributes to disease.
