課程目標   Learning Objectives
Through this class, we read paragraphs, and essays, by which we improve our grasp of grammar, increase our vocabulary, and learn how to analyze what we read.

We discuss questions in class, while there comes a final examination in the last week. Then you are supposed to answer questions relevant to our readings.


這門課是散文選讀 但是每次上課也會觸及寫作文的技巧
