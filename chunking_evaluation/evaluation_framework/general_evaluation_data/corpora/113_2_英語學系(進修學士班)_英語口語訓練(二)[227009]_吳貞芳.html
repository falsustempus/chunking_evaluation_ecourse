課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed to develop students’ English listening and speaking abilities and build confidence. Students will learn to discuss real ideas, current issues, express personal points of view, and deliver speech presentations.

At the end of the semester, students will be able to -
1. communicate and exchange information on various topics.
2. recognize elements of an effective presentation.
3. organize and execute presentations with PowerPoint.
