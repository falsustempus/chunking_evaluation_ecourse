課程目標   Learning Objectives
1. Students will be able to engage in critical and constructive oral exchanges of ideas and
concepts in peer, group, and class discussions in English.
2. Students will be able to make a great progress in spoken English regarding complexity,
accuracy, and fluency.
3. Students will be able to provide details and arguments when supporting a position in a
debate or a conversation.
