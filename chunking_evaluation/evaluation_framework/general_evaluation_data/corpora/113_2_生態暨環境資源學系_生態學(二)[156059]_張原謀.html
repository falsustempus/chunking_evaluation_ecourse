課程目標   Learning Objectives
本課程提供生態科學與環境科學的基礎認知，以及建構永續發展的基本學理。本課程讓學生學習生態學的基本觀念，學理，歷史背景，自然運作的原則，以及生態學理的應用。本課程是全學年課程，分為(一)(二)兩部份(上下學期)。內容包含：物理環境、個體生態學、族群生態學、物種互動、群落生態學、生態體系生態學、生態生物地理學、人類生態學等八個部分。生態學(二)是下學期的課程，包含群落生態學、生態體系生態學、生態生物地理學學等。
This course provides foundational knowledge in ecological and environmental sciences and builds the basic principles of sustainable development. Students will learn the fundamental concepts, theories, historical background, principles of natural processes, and applications of ecological theory. This is a full-year course divided into two parts, Ecology I (first semester) and Ecology II (second semester). The content includes eight sections: physical environment, individual ecology, population ecology, species interactions, community ecology, ecosystem ecology, ecological biogeography, and human ecology. Ecology II, offered in the second semester, covers community ecology, ecosystem ecology, and ecological biogeography.
