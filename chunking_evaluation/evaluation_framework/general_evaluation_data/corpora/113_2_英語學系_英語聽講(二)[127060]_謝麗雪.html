課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course will focus on an examination of the function of English passages, an interpretation of the intention of speakers, and an analysis of the organization and style of authors. Strategies taught in this course include: guessing meaning from context, increase listening and speaking ability, understanding meaning, making predictions in listening and making inferences. This course will also focus on listening and speaking. Students will be encouraged to express themselves on topics related to the texts and write reaction in English. They can learn more listening strategies and communication skills and improve their listening and speaking skills by doing group discussion and presentation.

成績評量方式補充說明   
1. Attendance is required. After the fourth absence, you will not have any grades for all the work in class. You will get whatever grade you have at that point for the course.
2. Attendance will be taken ten minutes after the bell rings. If you come later, you are absent. Do not come to give any excuse. Each hour (Two hours) of absence will result in a 0.5 (1)-point penalty of the participation grades. Each time of absence would cost 1 point of total grade. The time of lateness will be accumulated for losing total grade as being absence.
3. This policy will be implemented from the first day of class. If you register after day one, you will be considered absence for any class you miss before you register. If you don’t show up on day one, you are absent for that day.
4. The three situations: 6 leaves / 1 unexcused absence and 3 leaves / 2 unexcused absences will be equal to losing the chance of the final exam.
5. If you do not contribute to the group work or are absent for the exam or the group presentation, you will be given a grade of zero.
Requirements:

6. Each person needs to present, answer or ask Q voluntarily for a few times in order to get the points for the performance on participation, discussion and reports. Those who attend certain activities related to English will be given extra points.
7. From the first week on, you will need to sit on a fixed place for the whole semester.
8. The make-up work for the worksheets will get 70% of the grades offered. Handouts fee: $10.
9. Everyone needs a textbook for the course. If you forget to bring one, you will lose 0.5 total grade of the course each time.
10. The mid-term report is an individual-report activity. For the final exam, there will be a written test and an oral report section about a topic assigned in class and you have to record your speech and upload it to the e-course right away in class.
