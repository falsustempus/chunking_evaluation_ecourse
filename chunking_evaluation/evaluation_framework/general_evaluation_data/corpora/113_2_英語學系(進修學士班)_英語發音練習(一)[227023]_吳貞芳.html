課程目標   Learning Objectives
At the end of this course, students will be able to -
1. distinguish English vowels and consonants and pronounce each sound accurately.
2. use stress, rhythm, and intonation accurately while uttering English sentences.
3. improve and apply their pronunciation skills for effective communication.
4. learn basic concepts of English phonetics and pronunciation.
