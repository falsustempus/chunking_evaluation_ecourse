課程目標   Learning Objectives
A second-year writing course is essentially an advanced grammar and writing lesson. We tackle the key grammatical and lexical elements learners need to become more successful academic writers. This course is aimed to help students develop English language skills in different genre paragraphs.

1. Student will have a wide variety of editing activities to refines students' academic writing and improves accuracy.
2. Students will develop the ability to think critically in written English on different topics such as global issues, economics and the environment.
3. Students will learn how to find resources to enrich the content of their compositions.
4. Students will significantly foster corpus-informed vocabulary and use it in student writing.
5. Students will emphasize the most common errors found in student writing.
