課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed to help those who desire to develop the listening and spoken language which they need to communicate in their personal and work lives. Students will be required to engage with interactive practice activities form multimedia materials of the textbook. Additionally, students will be trained to build the collaborative and individual skills to apply for their needs.
