課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of system performance analysis. By utilizing fundamental mathematical tools and modeling techniques, students will develop the ability to analyze computer service and integrated software-hardware platforms. The course covers Markov chains, queueing models, and system performance evaluation methods to enhance students' analytical skills in system analysis. In the latter part of the course, paper studies and project designs will be employed to conduct practical performance evaluations.
