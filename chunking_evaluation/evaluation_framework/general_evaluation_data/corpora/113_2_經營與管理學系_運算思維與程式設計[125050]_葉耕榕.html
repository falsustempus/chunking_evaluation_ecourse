課程目標   Learning Objectives
The purpose of this course is to enhance students' understanding of information technology. This course has two sections as follows. First, it helps students understand how enterprises and/or organizations utilized digital technology, especially from the perspective of data storage and processing. And also why artificial intelligence and big data are more and more important today. Second, students can learn basic Python programming language and why programming language is the foundation of modern information technology. And also how to access online resources for Python self-learning. Through this course, students should be able to establish the fundamental understanding of computational thinking and programming.
