課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed to develop effective business writing skills that are essential for professional success. Students will learn to craft clear, concise, and impactful business communications, including emails, reports, and proposals. Emphasis will be placed on tone, style, and structure, along with the practical application of writing skills to real-world scenarios.

By the end of the course, students will be able to:
- Write clear and professional business documents, including emails, reports, and meeting notes.
- Use appropriate tone and style for various business situations.
- Organize and present information effectively in written formats.
- Understand and apply strategies for negotiating, handling complaints, and making recommendations in writing.
- Edit and proofread business documents for clarity and accuracy.
