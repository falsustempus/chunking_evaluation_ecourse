課程目標   Learning Objectives
After this course, students can:
(1)Understand the statistical applications in business and economics
(2)Get knowledge and operation about descriptive statistics, basic probabilities, discrete probabilities, continuous probabilities, interval estimation, hypothesis examination.
(3)Obtain the capabilities of the planning and implementation of statistical investigation, data collection, statistical analysis, and explanation.
