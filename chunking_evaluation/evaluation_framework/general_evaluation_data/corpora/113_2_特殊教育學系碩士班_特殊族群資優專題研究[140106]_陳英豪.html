課程目標   Learning Objectives
The course focus on topics such as gifted education in twice-exceptional children, gifted females, gifted and talented students on the underachievement, students from culturally or linguistically diverse backgrounds, highly gifted learners, and gifted young children.

children of extraordinarily high IQ. In N. Colangelo, & G. A. Davis (Eds.),
