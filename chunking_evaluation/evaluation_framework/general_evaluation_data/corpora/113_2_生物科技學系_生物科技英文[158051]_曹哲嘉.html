課程目標   Learning Objectives
The objective of this course is to help students develop the skills to use English as a tool for learning, communicating, and publishing in the field of basic biology and applied biotechnology. Students will be able to:

(1) Communicate and interact effectively in professional settings in the biotechnology field;
(2) Search for and find relevant information on the internet about life sciences and biotechnology;
(3) Read and understand professional literature;
(4) Give oral presentations.
