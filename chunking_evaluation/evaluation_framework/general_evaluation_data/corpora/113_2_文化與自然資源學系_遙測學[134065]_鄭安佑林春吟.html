課程目標   Learning Objectives
Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) are two essential disciplines in modern environmental and geographical sciences. They work closely together, providing powerful tools for spatial data processing and analysis, and are widely applied in areas such as environmental monitoring, urban planning, disaster management, and resource management. This course aims to introduce the basic concepts, technologies, and applications of Remote Sensing and GIS, exploring how they can be integrated to solve real-world geographical and spatial problems. The course content primarily focuses on the management, analysis, and visualization of spatial data using GIS software.
