課程目標   Learning Objectives

This course is designed to improve freshmen’s English communication skills. Students will acquire English proficiency through practices in class and the materials incorporating grammar knowledge, listening comprehension, and reading techniques. The overall English ability for daily use is enhanced. Various in-class activities and assignments are given to reach the teaching goal.

The aims of this course include the followings:
1. To improve freshmen’s English communication skills.
2. To improve students' English proficiency.
3. To enhance students' grammar knowledge, listening comprehension, and reading
techniques.
