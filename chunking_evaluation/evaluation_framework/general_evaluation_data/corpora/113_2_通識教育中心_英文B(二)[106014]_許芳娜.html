課程目標   Learning Objectives
The goal of this course is to make English unforgettable through
1. multiple exposure to new language
2. Numerous opportunities to practice it
3. deliberate and intensive recycling
which includes extra grammar exercises, digital full-color vocabulary flash cards, conversation activator videos, and pronunciation coach videos

本課程期以多元教學內容 不斷練習 重複密集使用新語言：如文法練習 豐富有去之線上單字卡練習 對話情境影片 發音指導影片達到學生有效學習之目標
