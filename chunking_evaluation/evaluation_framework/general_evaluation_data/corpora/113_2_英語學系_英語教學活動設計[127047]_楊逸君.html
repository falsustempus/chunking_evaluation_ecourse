課程目標   Learning Objectives
1. Students will have a basic understanding of language learning theories and how they are related to activity design of EFL learning.
2. Students will be able to design games, exercises, and activities which are related to and are beneficial to EFL young learners.
3. Students will be able to design lessons in EFL teaching which are well embedded with appropriate games, exercises, and activities to enhance language learning.

成績評量方式補充說明   
Course requirements:
1. No eating is allowed in class. If you bring any food to class and have it while the
session is going on, it will lead to the deduction of five points from your attendance and
participation subscore.
2. Students are fully responsible for all information and assignments missed due to
tardiness or absence.
3. Students who are absent for more than three sessions will receive a 30% lower
grade on attendance and participation.
4. You should be ready to spend 2-3 hours outside of class each week for this class.
5. Quizzes will be announced a week in advance.
6. Missing either the mid-term exam or the final will lead to your failing the class.
7. Please be aware that any plagiarism is not allowed in this class. Any copying and pasting texts from already available sources will lead to your failing the class as well.
8. The mid-term will be in written form while the final will be group presentations. All of the assignments will be in written form.
9. Students have to turn in their slides for the instructor's suggestions before formally presenting them in class. The slides have to be submitted one week before the presentation. Failing to do so will lead to the deduction of five points from the grade of the presentation.
