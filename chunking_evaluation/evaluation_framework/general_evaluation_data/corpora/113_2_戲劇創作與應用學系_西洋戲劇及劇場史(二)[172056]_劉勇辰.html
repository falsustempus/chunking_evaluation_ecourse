課程目標   Learning Objectives
The course aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the development of Western drama, exploring the evolution of theater from Ancient Greece and Rome to the modern era. The course will focus on dramatic works, playwrights, performance styles, and the development of theatrical aesthetics, examining how these elements reflect and influence the social and cultural contexts in which they exist. Students will learn to critically analyze Western drama, utilizing historical and cultural knowledge to interpret and understand the deeper meanings of theatrical works. In addition, the course will cultivate students' practical abilities in drama creation, directing, performance and theater production through reading original plays, observing video materials and group discussions, and stimulate their inspiration and creativity in using knowledge of theater history in the creative process.
