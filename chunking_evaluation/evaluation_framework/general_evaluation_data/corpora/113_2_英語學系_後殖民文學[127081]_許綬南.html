課程目標   Learning Objectives
What is postcolonialism? In this class we examine two novels from the postcolonial approach. As postcolonialism is still fashionable in some parts of the world, this class helps us better know where we live and who we are, as well as those people in other parts of the world who suffered from colonial rule.
Season of Migration to the North (an unforgetable book, by the Sudanese writer, Tayeb Salih) definitely belongs to postcolonial literature, while The English Patient (by the Canadian writer, Michael Ondaatje, born in Sri Lanka) has been approached in various ways. For example, it is very postmodern. I choose it because of the role Kip, an Indian, plays in it.(By the way, when you are reading The English Patient, pay attention to its language--Ondaatje is a wonderful poet.)
