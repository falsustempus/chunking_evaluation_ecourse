課程目標   Learning Objectives
The purpose of this course is to train English-major students to teach English reading and writing skills to young learners. Students will learn the principles, procedures, and current practices of teaching English reading and writing skills. They will also learn how to become an effective literacy teacher by understanding the essential foundations of literacy teaching and learning up-to-date strategies of literacy teaching.
