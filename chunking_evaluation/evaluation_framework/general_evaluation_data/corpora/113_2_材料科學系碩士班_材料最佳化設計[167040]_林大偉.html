課程目標   Learning Objectives
The goals of this course are that we hope students can understand the application of different optimal methods in science and engineering, such as the gradient based method, constrainted and un-constrainted problem, genetic algorithm, annealing simulation and so on. Educating students have the concept and knowledge to design and optimal the engineered system by optimal method. In addition, training students have the fundamental theory to improve the engineering technology. The self developed optimized interface will be introduced for the rapid and direct solution. Finally, the students can obtain the enough experiences through the complete optimized design based on the finite element model.
