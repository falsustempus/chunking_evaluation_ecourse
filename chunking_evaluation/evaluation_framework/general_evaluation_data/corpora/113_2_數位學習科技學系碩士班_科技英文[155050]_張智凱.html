課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed to help students in multimedia and information technology improve their English language skills, with a focus on understanding technical concepts and terminology related to the field. The course will likely cover various grammar topics, including reading and writing comprehension, summarizing, and report writing, as well as vocabulary development. It may also include a focus on specific technical concepts such as networks, distributed computing, and computer maintenance, as well as an overview of key concepts like the CPU, OS, and basic computer structure. Overall, the goal of the course is to help students effectively communicate in English about technical concepts and ideas within the field of multimedia and information technology.
