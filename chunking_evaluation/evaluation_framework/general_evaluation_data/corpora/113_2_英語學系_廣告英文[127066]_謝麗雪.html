課程目標   Learning Objectives
Advertising in English is about learning basic knowledge of current linguistic forms of advertising and cultivation of application of advertising language. This course combines the advertising application activities with theory and practice, and requires the following content. First, commercial is an important expressive method, which helps to understand language, so understanding the advertising language is important. Second, learning the analytical ability of advertising language helps to understand appropriate linguistic phenomena and avoid similar errors. Third, learning correct language in advertisement assists the application in the advertising service, so that practice can be applied with the theory and make the students’ advertising knowledge develop better and improve their life style of using products. After taking the course, students may improve their oral expressive ability and translation skills. They may also actively get in touch with more types of materials in learning English. Students may learn to cooperate with others more passionately after doing group tasks and a final report.

成績評量方式補充說明   
Class participation: 10%, comments: 5%, group discussion 15%, midterm exam 35% and final report 35%. An extra 0.1 point or more grades will be given for each voluntary response or performance in class. (the maximum is 5%). Each time of absence would cost 1 point of total grade. The time of lateness will be accumulated for losing total grade as being absence.

Requirements
1. Everyone needs to do tasks in class and out of class, which will be graded.
2. Some assigned tasks will be done after class and then you may pose questions to be answered in class.
3. Pair or group discussion and presentation will be done and recorded in class. Each one needs to speak for 5 times.
4. one point of the final grade of the course will be deducted for one hour absence (10 minutes/50 minutes late will be counted as one absence).
5. No cheating and no late work is allowed in this course.
6. Handout and worksheet fee: $60.
7. Each volunteer response will be added with 1/10 point of the final total grade after the fifth times. Any volunteer participation of a competition in English will gain 1 point for your total grade.
8. From the second week on, you will need to sit on a fixed place.

Guidelines for the final report
1. Form a group of 6 people (may be adjusted based on the class size)
2. Design a commercial film to introduce the English Department (or a designed product) (If you are not English major, you can choose to make a commercial film of your department or our school).
3. The commercial film should be made into a video with subtitles, pictures, dramas, music in a story (about 6 minutes).
4. For the presentation, you need to make a ppt to introduce your commercial and design concept (about 10 minutes and everyone needs to talk for about 1.5 minutes), and play the commercial video to the class.
