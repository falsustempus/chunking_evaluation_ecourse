課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course offers an in‐depth exploration of the fundamental principles, mechanisms, and architectures associated with cloud computing, covering topics such as virtualization, software‐defined networking (SDN), edge computing, fog computing, quality of service
(QoS), and service‐level agreements (SLA). The course will incorporate AWS Academy as a hands-on practice platform for Cloud Computing. This will help students become familiar with potential work environments they may encounter in their future careers.
