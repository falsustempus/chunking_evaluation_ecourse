課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course introduces students to the study of word structure and word formation. It focuses on three fundamental aspects of morphology: morphological analysis, derivational and inflectional morphology, morphology and its relation to other linguistic levels (e.g. phonology, syntax, and semantics). At the end of the course, students will be expected to build skills in analyzing language data, and to gain knowledge related to words in increasing their vocabulary while learning foreign languages.
