課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is to introduce the basic management theories and concepts, including management functions such as business environment analysis, planning, organizing, leading, controlling, and integrative management issues including international management and entrepreneurship. Through empirical articles, the course intends to encourage students to apply the theories to the real world. 

先修 ( 前置 ) 課程   Prerequisite
Lecture and discussion will be used primarily. Empirical examples will be applied during the course. Students will also be assigned HBR articles presentation.
