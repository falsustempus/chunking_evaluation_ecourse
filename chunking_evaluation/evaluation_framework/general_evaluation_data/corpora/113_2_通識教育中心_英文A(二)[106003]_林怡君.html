課程目標   Learning Objectives
This class focuses on reading, writing, speaking, and listening.
In this class, students are asked to read texts with a wide range of topics and they are supposed to do their group work during the class. By asking students to do their group work, this class also hopes students can stimulate their writing skill. Except for reading and listening, presenting in English is also the key point for this course. Moreover, some of the English proficiency tests, such as TOEIC, will be introduced to students in this semester.
