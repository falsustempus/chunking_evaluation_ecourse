課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is aimed to provide students with a practical guide for developing materials for EFL learners. Students will be introduced to some theories about language materials development and apply those theories in L2 materials development. They will also be introduced to some existing software, be familiar with the application of those software, and develop language and teaching materials through the use of those software.
