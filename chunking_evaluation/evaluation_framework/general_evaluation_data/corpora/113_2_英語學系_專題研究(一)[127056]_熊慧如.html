課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course trains students in the essential skills of linguistics research, including designing, conducting, and presenting projects. Students will learn to collect and analyze language data, apply theoretical frameworks, and utilize AI tools to enhance research. The course also emphasizes academic writing and collaborative skills, preparing students for advanced study or professional applications in linguistics and related fields.
