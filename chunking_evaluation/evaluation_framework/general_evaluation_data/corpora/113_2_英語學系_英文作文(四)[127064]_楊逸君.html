課程目標   Learning Objectives
1. Students’ language awareness regarding this gap between what they can write and what they want to write in English will be significantly enhanced.
2. Students will learn how to plan in academic English writing and how to find resources to enrich the content of their compositions.
3. Students’ three dimensions of EFL writing, namely complexity, accuracy, and fluency, will be significantly fostered.
4. Students will develop the ability to think critically in written English when writing on topics related to global issues, such as economics and environment.
