課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed to help those who are eager to develop the listening and spoken language skills which they need to communicate in their personal and work lives. Students will be required to engage with interactive practice activities from multimedia materials and grammatical sentence usage of the textbook. Furthermore, students will be trained to build the language skills through collaborative and self practice and apply for their needs.
