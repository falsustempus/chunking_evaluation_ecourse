課程目標   Learning Objectives
本課程為對線性代數之內容及其應用作學習. 課程(二)包括 Vector Spaces, Bases and Dimension, Determinants , Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors, Diagonalization, Linear Transformations, Representations.
