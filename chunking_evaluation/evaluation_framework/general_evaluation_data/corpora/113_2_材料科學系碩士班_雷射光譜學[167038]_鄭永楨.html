課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course covers the topics of lectures are principles of lasers in a nutshell, light beams and pulses, optical resonators, light interaction with atomic systems, etc. It is hoped that this course may help to students. Although it is meant as an introduction to laser spectroscopy, it may also facilitate the understanding of advanced papers on special subjects in laser spectroscopy.
