課程目標   Learning Objectives
1.Students will be able to comprehend English conversations on day-to-day topics.
2.Students will have the ability to use simple English sentences to describe common objects and events, converse with others, and engage in group discussions.
3.Students will demonstrate competence in reading short English essays, personal letters, advertisements, and the like.
4.Students will acquire skills to write short English essays and personal letters.
5.Students will develop critical thinking skills in order to prepare for and deliver professional oral presentations in English by summarizing key information in list format.
