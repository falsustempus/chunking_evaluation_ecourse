課程目標   Learning Objectives
1. The student will engage in thorough study and research on a chosen topic, resulting in a project that significantly contributes to the field. Each student will have Regular meetings with the instructor to discuss progress, make revisions, and reorganize the project as needed.

2. The specific research objectives will vary for each student and will be determined collaboratively by the student and the instructor.

3. This class will provide students with the opportunity to explore a topic of personal interest in-depth, under the guidance of an instructor who will oversee and evaluate the student's progress.
