課程目標   Learning Objectives
LECTURE EXAMINATIONS
Exam questions will be short answer to essay and will primarily come from the lecture. Study questions for each lecture will be posted on the homepage. Exam dates are given in the Semester Schedule.
MISSED EXAMINATIONS
Students will be permitted to make up exams with a significant, documented excuse (generally health related, see NUTN Policy Statement), which should be submitted to me by 48 hrs before the exam. Requests for make up exams made after the exam will only be granted for extreme emergencies. You must contact me to request a makeup exam by no later than 24 hours after the time the exam is given or you will receive a grade of zero.
