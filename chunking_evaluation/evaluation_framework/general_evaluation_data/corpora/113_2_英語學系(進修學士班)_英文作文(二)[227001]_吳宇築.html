課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course will cover the basic elements of effective writing, with a particular emphasis on paragraph development and essay structure. Students will learn about different types of paragraphs, the characteristics of good writing, and how to organize their thoughts into well-structured essays.
This course aims to equip students with the fundamental skills of effective writing, focusing on paragraph development and essay structure. Through in-depth exploration of paragraph types, features of good writing, and the classification essay, students will develop the ability to articulate ideas clearly, logically, and persuasively. By the end of the course, students will be able to write well-structured paragraphs and coherent essays that demonstrate critical thinking and effective communication.
