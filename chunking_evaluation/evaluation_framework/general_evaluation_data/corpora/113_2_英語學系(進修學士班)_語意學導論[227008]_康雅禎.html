課程目標   Learning Objectives
The aim of this course is to seeks to understand the connections and interactions between the symbols for things and the things them selves. Upon completion of this course, students are expected to be able to(1) observe the relation between linguistic expressions and objects the words refer to and (2) describe sense properties and sense relations.
