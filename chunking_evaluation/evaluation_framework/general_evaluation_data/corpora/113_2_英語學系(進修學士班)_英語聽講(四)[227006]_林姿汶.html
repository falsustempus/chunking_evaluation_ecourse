課程目標   Learning Objectives
本課程提供英文在不同情境中的語言使用，如旅遊、觀光、餐廳美食、及生活應用，以強化應用在國際溝通場合之英文技能，並針對英文基本技能，如單字、聽力訓練、真實會話，以培養口語所需之英文表達及互動能力。希望學生修習完本課程後懂得如何規劃適合自己的自助旅行行程。

This course will
1. focus on practice of different types of language uses to discuss travelling, making requests, restaurant and daily conversation.
2. provide learners with language essentials, life skills and learner development to ensure that they are fully prepared and empowered to function effectively within the global world.
3. include essential English skills: language focus, vocabulary, and listening comprehension in each unit in order to provide interactive skills and expressions for oral presentation.
