課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course covers advanced topics in fluid mechanics, focusing on external flows, compressible flows, and an introduction to computational fluid dynamics (CFD). It builds on fundamental principles, emphasizing their application in solving practical engineering problems and understanding complex flow phenomena.
