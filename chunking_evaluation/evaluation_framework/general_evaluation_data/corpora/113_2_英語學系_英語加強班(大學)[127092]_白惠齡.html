課程目標   Learning Objectives
This course is designed based on the goal of helping learners be equipped with English communication skills at work. Through this course, students can be elevated in the four skills of English. Also, this course specifically focuses on improving students' listening, vocabulary, and reading abilities. Students will also learn some skills which can prepare them to survive in the workplace. In the course, students will be given practice on simulated situations at work. They will practice exactly the language, skills, and strategies they need when they are in the workplace. Through practicing the exercises, students can also acquire new vocabulary and related phrases.

成績評量方式補充說明   
1. 平時作業包含「口說」與「寫作」兩者均衡 (例如: 課本聽讀作業，外國網紅短影片，老師補充資料等)，以課堂指定為主。
2. 期初、期末煩請授課教師協助修課學生填寫線上自我評量表， 共計填寫 2 次自我評量，用以了解學生個別化學習歷程，線上連結：https://reurl.cc/G4pZMx
3. 本校學則相關規定為第二十二條，曠課一小時，作缺課三小時 論，缺課累積達總時數三分之一者，不得參加學期考試，該科以零分計算。
4. 若學生連續選修 2 學期英語加強班，每學期缺課累計達全學期授課總時數三分之一者(即每學期缺課逾(含)12 小時者)，則下一學期不得選修英語加強班。
5. 教科用書English for Work，請至本校復文書局購買。
