課程目標   Learning Objectives
At the end of this course, students will be able to -
1. expand their vocabulary by working on practice that reinforce vocabulary meaning in context.
2. improve their reading skills from materials featuring relevant and contemporary topics.
3. apply practical language skills in their everyday lives.
4. think critically about current issues.

在本課程結束時，學生將能夠 -
1. 學習由英文同義字或語境上下文理解推敲詞彙的意義，而非依賴中文翻譯學單字，持續增加詞彙量。
2. 透過閲讀當代主題文本提高閱讀能力。
3. 在日常生活中運用語言技能。
4. 對當代議題進行批判性思考。
