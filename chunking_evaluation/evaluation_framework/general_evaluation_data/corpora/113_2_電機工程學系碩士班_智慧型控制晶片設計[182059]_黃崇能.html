課程目標   Learning Objectives

Arduino is an open-source hardware platform from Italy that includes a board with simple I/O capabilities and a suite of programming environment software. Arduino can be used to develop interactive products, such as it can read a large number of switch and sensor signals, and can control lights, motors and a variety of other physical equipment; Arduino can also develop peripheral devices connected to the PC. It can communicate with the software on the PC at runtime.
This course is designed to give students a clearer understanding and understanding of the process of electromechanical integration through Arduino's practical design.
