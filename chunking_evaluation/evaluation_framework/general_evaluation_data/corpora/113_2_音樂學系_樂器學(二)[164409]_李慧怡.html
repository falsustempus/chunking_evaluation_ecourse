課程目標   Learning Objectives


1. 培養學生對於不同樂器的基本認識和理解，包括樂器的種類、結構、音域、移調，以及常見的演奏技巧。
2. 欣賞各個樂器在交響樂團中的角色，透過經驗分享，了解其音色和特性。
3. 使學生在修完課程後能深入瞭解西洋樂器與樂團曲目，並且認識台灣以及世界知名的演奏家。

相較於艱澀的英文單字和語彙，樂器學的英文是相對簡單的，
此堂課能幫助學生統整已有的英文能力

Survey of Musical Instruments is the class to help students recognize their musical instruments. The students will learn how to transpose the key with different instruments. In the class, the students will listen to the famous repertoires for knowing the range of instruments, the function of the sound projection, and the basic technique. Through this class, students will be able to recognize the most famous performers nowadays and be inspired by their performances. The purpose of this class is to understand the character of the instrument in the orchestra. By knowing the English repertoires, it helps students study professionally in their major.
